Bob's trip in hell

Explaining the game

Controls

Use your mouse to kill demons.

Items explained

Each round, you can select items(by clicking on them), that have energy costs.
You will have a total of 100 energy, gaining 10 energy each round.


Items that cost can be trashed for some as well, if you are running low.
While an item is equipped, you can see its' stats above Bob.
There are a total of 3 item types:
-Attack
-Damage
-Potion
