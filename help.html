Bob's trip in hell

Explaining the game

Story

You take on a journey, with Bob, who is an avarage teen.
One day he decided to use his set of VR goggles to play some games.
However when he touched the floor, he felt that he wasn't in his room anymore.
He started sweating as it got really hot wherever he was, with the goggles stuck to his head.
This is his (totally accurate) story of how he got home.

Objective

There are 15 rounds in total, with 3 bosses, one every 5 levels.
As your demon enemies get stronger each few rounds, you have to start thinking a bit.
Use your mouse to kill everything that you see. Is it really that simple? Kind of.
But be careful, there are no second chances.
Once you die, you are going to have to start over.
If Bob managed to do it first try, it can't be that hard. Right?

Equipped Items and Health

Each round, you can select items (by clicking on them), that have energy costs.
You will have a total of 120 energy, gaining 20 energy each round.


Items can be trashed as well, if you don't like your current ones.
While an item is equipped, you can see its' stats above Bob.
There are a total of 3 item types:
-Attack
-Damage booster
-Potion

Enemies' health can be seen under them, right next to their names.
